About Valentina

Valentina Elore is an author and creator at Visionary Rebel Press. Her work spans relationship clarity, archetypal patterning, and practical sovereignty. Through books, guides, and workshops, she helps readers decode manipulative dynamics and return to self-trust.

Current projects include: The Inner Compass Blueprint™, The Spiritual Fckboy Field Guide™, The Spiritual QueenPin Field Guide™, and La Brújula de la Sombra™.

Archetype: The Witchy Philosopher Heroine

Stars, moons, sage, and Ancient Latin incantations. A mirror in one hand and a wand in the other. Valentina moves between the seen and unseen, translating symbols into choices you can actually make.
